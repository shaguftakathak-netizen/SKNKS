About Us

At Shagufta Kathak Nritya Kala Samiti, we bring classical finesse and modern pedagogy together. From Kathak to folk, Bollywood, and contemporary, our online classes are designed for all ages and levels.

Our director has been honoured internationally as an “International Kathak Dance Principal.” Learn from the best and perform with confidence on any stage.
